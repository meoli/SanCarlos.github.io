St. Dominic Parish Church

St. Dominic de Guzman Parish Church is a Roman Catholic church located in the City of San Carlos, Pangasinan, Philippines. The church, made out of bricks or ladrillo, used to be the largest Catholic church in the Philippines during the late 18th century.

World's Largest Mango Pie

San Carlos City, the largest city of the Province of Pangasinan, set a world record when its mango pie became the world's largest mango pie last 2011.
